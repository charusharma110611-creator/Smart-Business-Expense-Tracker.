Project Name:- " Smart Business Manager "



     Problem Statement:-
           Small business and shop owners manage inventory and expenses manually in a book, which leads to errors, poor tracking, and 
           difficulty in understanding profit or remaining budget also mostely unable to find the low stock products.

     
     Proposed Solution:-
           Smart Business Manager is a web-based application that provides a simple and efficient way to manage:
              -> Product Inventory
              -> Business expenses
              -> Remaining money and overall financial overview
                  All data is stored digitally in a database for reliability and easy access.


      Key Objectives:-
       -To digitize inventory and expense tracking
       -To reduce manual calculation errors
       -To provide a clear dashboard view of business data
       -To make business management simple and user-friendly


      Core Features:
        -Dashboard showing total products, total expenses, and remaining money
        -Inventory management: add, view, and delete products
        -Expense management: add, view, and delete expenses
        -Automatic calculation of totals and remaining balance
        -Low stock product identification
        -Persistent data storage using SQLite database


     Technology Stack:
      _Backend: Python (Flask)
      _Frontend: HTML, CSS
      _Database: SQLite (Flask-SQLAlchemy)


     Target Users:
       -"Small shop owners"
       -"Local businesses"
       -"Students learning business management"
       -"Anyone who wants a simple business tracking system"


     Innovation / Impact:
       *Replaces manual registers with a digital system
       *Reduces errors in calculation and record keeping
       *Saves time and improves decision-making using clear data visualization
       *Can be extended into a full business management system in the future


     Scalability / Future Scope:
       >>..Add user authentication (multi-user support)
       >>..Add charts and analytics for better insights
       >>..Add report export (PDF/CSV)
       >>..Add edit/update functionality for products and expenses
